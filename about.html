About Us

A family-owned company built on trust and personal care.

Our Story

BH Cleaning SF was founded in 2019 by Brenda Goes with a simple mission: provide dependable, high-quality cleaning while treating every property with care and respect.

As the company grew, her husband Halesson joined the business. Together, they now lead a team of eight professionals while maintaining the personal approach that clients have trusted since the beginning.

Brenda personally visits every new client before service begins. She learns the property's priorities and accompanies the team during the first cleaning to ensure the plan is followed correctly.
